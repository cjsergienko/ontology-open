# ML Infrastructure Engineer

**Location:** Chicago, IL
**Employment Type:** Full time
**Level:** Senior

---

## About This Role

The role focuses on architecting and scaling ML platform infrastructure for
model training, deployment, and monitoring. The engineer will address
distributed systems challenges including GPU optimization, scalable model
serving pipelines, and observability tooling to maintain production
reliability at scale.

**Core Technology Stack**
- Languages & Frameworks: Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, MLflow
- Infrastructure: Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform
- Cloud: AWS, GCP, Azure (multi-cloud)
- Monitoring: Prometheus, Grafana, Datadog

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Design distributed training infrastructure for large-scale workloads with
  GPU scheduling optimization across Kubernetes clusters
- Build scalable model serving pipelines achieving sub-100ms inference
  latency with autoscaling capabilities
- Establish monitoring, alerting, and debugging infrastructure maintaining
  99.9% uptime
- Evaluate and prototype emerging MLOps patterns and deployment technologies
- Create automation tooling for data scientists covering hyperparameter
  tuning, versioning, and A/B testing
- Collaborate with ML researchers and data engineers to translate workflow
  needs into technical specifications
- Drive architectural platform decisions with minimal oversight

---

## Required Qualifications

- 5–8 years professional software engineering experience building
  production infrastructure
- Expert-level Python for building robust, production-grade systems that
  teams can maintain
- Deep Kubernetes production experience including cluster architecture
  design
- Strong distributed systems knowledge with fault tolerance and
  observability design
- Proficiency in PyTorch or TensorFlow with training and inference
  optimization understanding
- Docker and infrastructure-as-code (Terraform) production experience
- Proven track record architecting platform components independently
- Strong analytical and debugging capabilities

---

## Preferred Qualifications

- MLOps platform experience (MLflow, Kubeflow, Metaflow)
- GPU utilization optimization and distributed training expertise
- Experience in regulated domains requiring model reliability and
  auditability
- Open-source contributions to ML infrastructure projects
- LLM infrastructure patterns knowledge (vLLM, TGI, quantization, serving)
