# Platform Engineer

**Location:** Austin, TX
**Employment Type:** Full time
**Level:** Senior

---

## Job Summary

The company seeks an experienced infrastructure specialist to design and
maintain cloud-native infrastructure supporting multiple engineering teams.
The role emphasizes system reliability, developer experience, and scaling
challenges.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Manage platform reliability through monitoring, alerting, and incident
  response
- Design cloud infrastructure using Kubernetes, Terraform, and AWS services
- Build CI/CD pipelines reducing deployment friction
- Analyze production performance using observability data
- Evaluate and implement emerging cloud-native technologies
- Collaborate with teams to translate infrastructure needs into platform
  features
- Mentor engineers on infrastructure best practices

---

## Required Qualifications

- 6–8 years production infrastructure engineering experience
- Deep Kubernetes expertise including multi-cluster deployments
- Strong Terraform proficiency with infrastructure-as-code patterns
- Hands-on AWS experience (VPCs, IAM, ECS/EKS, RDS, S3)
- CI/CD pipeline development experience
- Programming fluency in Python, Go, or Bash
- Strong analytical problem-solving for distributed systems
- Self-directed ownership of infrastructure projects

---

## Preferred Qualifications

- Observability tooling experience (Prometheus, Grafana, Datadog, ELK)
- GitOps workflows or service mesh architecture knowledge
- Experience scaling infrastructure from startup to enterprise

---

## Required Skills

Kubernetes, Terraform, AWS, Docker, Python, CI/CD, Observability
